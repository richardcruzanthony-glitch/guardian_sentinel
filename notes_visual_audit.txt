VISUAL AUDIT — Guardian Sentinel
=================================

HEADER:
- Domain tabs look like basic pill buttons — no visual weight or hierarchy
- "Request Demo" is plain text, "Early Access" is a green button — inconsistent pairing
- Logo area is fine but tagline "Learns · Recommends · Alerts · Executes" is small and forgettable

HERO SECTION:
- "Every Department. Simultaneously." — good copy but the typography is flat
- The green-on-dark text lacks depth — no gradient, no glow, no visual drama
- Body text below is just gray paragraph text — reads like a README, not a product pitch
- No visual element (illustration, animation, abstract graphic) to anchor the hero

COMPARISON BOXES:
- Left box (Manual Handoff) — plain numbered list with red time values, looks like a basic table
- Right box (Neural Architecture) — department badges are tiny green pills, hard to read
- The boxes have thin borders but no visual distinction — they blend into the dark background
- "All fire simultaneously — seconds, not weeks" is buried in the box
- The three bullet points below are small and forgettable
- No visual energy — this is the KEY selling point and it looks like a sidebar

UPLOAD SECTION:
- "Upload Engineering Drawing" header is small and plain
- Dashed border drop zone is the default pattern — looks like every file upload ever
- "Drawing preview will appear here" placeholder is just gray text in an empty box — looks broken
- Material dropdown and Quantity input are basic form elements with no styling
- The "Ara — Coordinate All Departments" button is a full-width green bar — looks like a generic submit button, not a premium AI action

CTA SECTION:
- "Ready to Transform Your Operations?" — decent copy but the section is plain
- Two buttons side by side look basic
- Contact info (Richard Cruz, phone) is just plain text at the bottom — no visual treatment

FOOTER:
- Minimal and fine, but the tagline repeats the header — feels redundant

OVERALL ISSUES:
1. No depth — everything is flat. No shadows, no gradients, no layering
2. No visual anchors — no illustrations, icons with meaning, or abstract graphics
3. Typography is monotone — same weight, same style throughout
4. The comparison section (the most important part) doesn't command attention
5. Upload area looks like a dev prototype, not a polished product
6. No background variation — solid dark everywhere creates visual fatigue
7. The green accent color is used for everything — no hierarchy in color usage
8. Form elements are unstyled defaults
9. No micro-interactions or visual feedback
10. The page reads top-to-bottom like a document, not a product experience
